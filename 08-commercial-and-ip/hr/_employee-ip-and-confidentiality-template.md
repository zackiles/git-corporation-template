---

## Recitals

A. The Corporation has offered employment to the Employee.

B. In the course of employment, the Employee may create intellectual property and receive confidential information.

C. The parties wish to establish their respective rights regarding IP and confidentiality.

---

## 1. Definitions

- **"Confidential Information"** means non-public information relating to the Corporation's business, including customer lists, financial information, technical data, business strategies, and trade secrets.

- **"Work Product"** means all inventions, works, ideas, designs, software, documentation, and other materials created by the Employee in the course of employment.

---

## 2. Confidentiality

### 2.1 Non-Disclosure

The Employee agrees to:
- Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party
- Not use Confidential Information for any purpose other than performing duties
- Protect Confidential Information using reasonable care

### 2.2 Exceptions

These obligations do not apply to information that:
- Becomes publicly available through no fault of the Employee
- Is required to be disclosed by law

### 2.3 Return of Information

Upon termination, the Employee shall immediately return all Confidential Information and Corporation property.

### 2.4 Survival

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of employment indefinitely for trade secrets and for [NUMBER] years for other Confidential Information.

---

## 3. Intellectual Property

### 3.1 Assignment of Work Product

The Employee hereby assigns to the Corporation all right, title, and interest in:
- All Work Product created during employment
- All intellectual property rights in the Work Product

### 3.2 Waiver of Moral Rights

The Employee waives all moral rights in the Work Product.

### 3.3 Prior Inventions

The Employee has listed in Schedule A any prior inventions the Employee wishes to exclude from this agreement.

### 3.4 Disclosure

The Employee shall promptly disclose all Work Product to the Corporation.

### 3.5 Further Assurances

The Employee agrees to execute any documents and provide any assistance necessary to perfect the Corporation's ownership.

---

## 4. Non-Competition

[Include if permitted by applicable law]

During employment and for [NUMBER] months after termination, the Employee shall not:
- Work for a competitor in a similar role
- Solicit customers or employees of the Corporation

---

## 5. General

### 5.1 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement regarding IP and confidentiality.

### 5.2 Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [PROVINCE] and Canada.

### 5.3 Severability

If any provision is unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in effect.

### 5.4 Injunctive Relief

The Employee acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm and the Corporation may seek injunctive relief.
