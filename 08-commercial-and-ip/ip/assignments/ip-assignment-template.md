---

## Recitals

A. The Assignor has developed or owns certain intellectual property.

B. The Corporation wishes to acquire ownership of such intellectual property.

C. The Assignor has agreed to assign the intellectual property to the Corporation.

---

## 1. Definitions

- **"Assigned IP"** means the intellectual property described in Schedule A.
- **"Intellectual Property Rights"** means all patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and other intellectual property rights.

---

## 2. Assignment

### 2.1 Assignment of IP

The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns to the Corporation all right, title, and interest in and to:
- The Assigned IP
- All Intellectual Property Rights in the Assigned IP
- All rights to sue for past infringement

### 2.2 Waiver of Moral Rights

The Assignor hereby waives all moral rights in the Assigned IP.

### 2.3 Future Works

The Assignor assigns all future improvements, modifications, and derivative works of the Assigned IP.

---

## 3. Consideration

In consideration for this assignment, the Corporation shall [pay the Assignor $[AMOUNT] / issue [NUMBER] shares to the Assignor / acknowledge the Assignor's existing obligations].

---

## 4. Representations and Warranties

The Assignor represents and warrants that:
- The Assignor is the sole owner of the Assigned IP
- The Assigned IP does not infringe any third party rights
- No third party has any claim to the Assigned IP
- The Assignor has full authority to make this assignment

---

## 5. Further Assurances

The Assignor agrees to:
- Execute any documents necessary to perfect the assignment
- Assist with patent, copyright, or trademark applications
- Cooperate in any legal proceedings regarding the Assigned IP

---

## 6. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [PROVINCE] and Canada.
