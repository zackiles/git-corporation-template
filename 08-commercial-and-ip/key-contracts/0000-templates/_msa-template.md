## 1. Definitions

- **"Agreement"** means this Master Service Agreement and all Statements of Work.
- **"Confidential Information"** means non-public information disclosed by either party.
- **"Deliverables"** means the work product to be delivered under a Statement of Work.
- **"Services"** means the services described in each Statement of Work.
- **"Statement of Work" or "SOW"** means a document describing specific services.

---

## 2. Services

### 2.1 Engagement

The Company agrees to provide Services as described in Statements of Work executed by both parties.

### 2.2 Statements of Work

Each SOW shall include:
- Description of Services
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Fees and payment terms
- Acceptance criteria

### 2.3 Performance

The Company shall perform Services in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.

### 3.1 Fees

The Client shall pay the fees specified in each SOW.

### 3.4 Expenses

[Option B: License]

## 5. Confidentiality

### 5.1 Obligations

Each party agrees to:
- Protect the other's Confidential Information
- Use Confidential Information only for purposes of this Agreement
- Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties

### 5.2 Exceptions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that is:
- Publicly available
- Already known to the receiving party
- Received from a third party without restriction
- Required to be disclosed by law
