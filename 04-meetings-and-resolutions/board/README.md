# Board Meetings and Resolutions

This folder contains minutes and resolutions from meetings of the Board of Directors.

## Contents

- `0000-templates/` - Templates for board meeting documents
- `YYYY/` - Year folders containing meetings and resolutions

## Templates

- `board-meeting-minutes-template.md` - Template for recording meeting minutes
- `board-resolution-template.md` - Template for individual resolutions

## Creating a New Meeting Record

1. Create folder: `YYYY/YYYY-MM-DD-description/`
2. Copy templates and complete with meeting details
3. Store signed copies in `09-binary-artifacts/04-meetings-and-resolutions/board/YYYY/`
