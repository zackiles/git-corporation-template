## Article 1 – Interpretation

### 1.1 Definitions

In this by-law:

- **"Act"** means the Canada Business Corporations Act and regulations thereunder, as amended.
- **"Articles"** means the articles of incorporation of the Corporation, as amended.
- **"Board"** means the board of directors of the Corporation.
- **"Corporation"** means [CORPORATION NAME].
- **"Director"** means a director of the Corporation.
- **"Officer"** means an officer of the Corporation.
- **"Shareholder"** means a shareholder of the Corporation.

---

## Article 2 – Corporate Name and Registered Office

### 2.1 Name

The name of the Corporation is: [CORPORATION NAME]

### 2.2 Registered Office

The registered office of the Corporation shall be at such place in Canada as the Board may determine.

---

## Article 3 – Directors

### 3.1 Number

The Board shall consist of a minimum of [NUMBER] and a maximum of [NUMBER] directors.

### 3.2 Qualifications

A director need not be a shareholder. At least [NUMBER] of the directors shall be resident Canadians.

### 3.3 Election and Term

Directors shall be elected at each annual meeting of shareholders and shall hold office until the next annual meeting or until their successors are elected.

### 3.4 Removal

Shareholders may remove a director by ordinary resolution at a special meeting called for that purpose.

### 3.5 Vacancies

Subject to the Act, a vacancy on the Board may be filled by resolution of the remaining directors.

### 3.6 Powers

The Board shall manage or supervise the management of the business and affairs of the Corporation.

---

## Article 4 – Officers

### 4.1 Appointment

The Board may appoint officers including a President, Secretary, Treasurer, and such other officers as the Board determines.

### 4.2 Duties

Officers shall have such duties as the Board may prescribe.

### 4.3 Removal

The Board may remove any officer at any time, with or without cause.

---

## Article 5 – Meetings of Directors

### 5.1 Place of Meetings

Meetings of the Board may be held at any place within or outside Canada.

### 5.2 Calling Meetings

Meetings of the Board may be called by the Chair, President, or any two directors.

### 5.3 Notice

Notice of meetings shall be given at least [NUMBER] hours before the meeting. No notice is required if all directors consent.

### 5.4 Quorum

A majority of the directors constitutes a quorum.

### 5.5 Voting

Each director has one vote. Decisions are made by majority vote.

### 5.6 Resolutions in Writing

A resolution in writing signed by all directors is as valid as if passed at a meeting.

---

## Article 6 – Meetings of Shareholders

### 6.1 Annual Meetings

The annual meeting of shareholders shall be held within [NUMBER] months after the end of each fiscal year.

### 6.2 Special Meetings

Special meetings may be called by the Board or by shareholders holding at least [PERCENTAGE]% of shares.

### 6.3 Notice

Notice of meetings shall be given at least [NUMBER] days but not more than [NUMBER] days before the meeting.

### 6.4 Quorum

Shareholders holding at least [PERCENTAGE]% of shares entitled to vote constitute a quorum.

### 6.5 Voting

Each share entitled to vote carries one vote. Decisions are made by majority vote unless otherwise required by the Act.

### 6.6 Resolutions in Writing

A resolution in writing signed by all shareholders entitled to vote is as valid as if passed at a meeting.

---

## Article 7 – Banking and Borrowing

### 7.1 Banking

The Board shall designate the bankers of the Corporation and authorize signing officers.

### 7.2 Borrowing

Subject to the Articles and any unanimous shareholder agreement, the Board may:
- Borrow money on the credit of the Corporation
- Issue debt obligations
- Mortgage, charge, or pledge assets as security

---

## Article 8 – Dividends

### 8.1 Declaration

The Board may declare dividends payable to shareholders according to their rights.

### 8.2 Solvency Test

No dividend shall be declared if there are reasonable grounds to believe the Corporation would be unable to pay its liabilities as they become due.

---

## Article 9 – Shares and Share Certificates

### 9.1 Issue of Shares

Shares may be issued at such times and to such persons as the Board determines.

### 9.2 Share Certificates

Share certificates shall be in a form approved by the Board and signed by at least one director or officer.

### 9.3 Transfer of Shares

Shares may be transferred in the manner provided by the Act and subject to any restrictions in the Articles.

---

## Article 10 – Indemnification

### 10.1 Indemnification of Directors and Officers

The Corporation shall indemnify directors and officers to the fullest extent permitted by the Act.

### 10.2 Insurance

The Corporation may purchase insurance for the benefit of directors and officers.

---

## Article 11 – Notices

### 11.1 Method

Notices may be given personally, by mail, by fax, or by electronic means.

### 11.2 Deemed Receipt

Notices are deemed received according to the Act.

---

## Article 12 – Execution of Documents

### 12.1 Signing Authority

Documents may be signed by such persons and in such manner as the Board may authorize.

### 12.2 Corporate Seal

The Corporation may have a corporate seal, but is not required to have one.

---

## Article 13 – Fiscal Year

The fiscal year of the Corporation ends on [DATE].

---

## Article 14 – Amendment

This by-law may be amended or repealed by resolution of the Board, subject to confirmation by shareholders.
