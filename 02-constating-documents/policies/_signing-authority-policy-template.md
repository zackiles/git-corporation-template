### 3.1 Single Signature

| Transaction Type | Limit |
|-----------------|-------|
| Routine operating expenses | Up to $[AMOUNT] |
| Vendor payments | Up to $[AMOUNT] |

### 3.2 Dual Signature Required

The following require two authorized signatories:

| Transaction Type | Limit |
|-----------------|-------|
| Payments | $[AMOUNT] to $[AMOUNT] |
| Capital expenditures | Over $[AMOUNT] |
| Real property transactions | Any amount |
| Borrowing/credit facilities | Over $[AMOUNT] |

- Transactions exceeding $[AMOUNT]

| Account Type | Signing Requirement |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Operating accounts | Any one of [NAMES] up to $[AMOUNT]; two required above |
| Payroll account | [NAME] only |

### 4.3 Wire Transfers

---

## 5. Document Types

### 5.1 Legal Documents

- Contracts, agreements, and amendments
- Certificates and attestations
- Regulatory filings

### 5.2 Financial Documents

- Cheques and payment authorizations
- Loan documents
- Tax filings

### 5.3 Corporate Documents

- Board and shareholder resolutions
- Share certificates
- Annual returns

---

## 6. Restrictions

### 6.1 Prohibited Actions

No signatory may:
- Sign documents creating personal benefit without Board approval
- Sign related party transactions without disclosure
- Exceed their authority limits

### 6.2 Conflicts of Interest

Signatories with conflicts must disclose and recuse themselves.

---

## 7. Record Keeping

All signed documents must be:
- Filed in the appropriate corporate records
- Logged in the signing register (if applicable)
- Copied to relevant parties

---

## 8. Review

This policy shall be reviewed annually by the Board.
