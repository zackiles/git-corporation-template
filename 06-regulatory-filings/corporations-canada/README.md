# Corporations Canada Filings

Filings with the federal corporate registry (Corporations Canada / ISED).

## Contents

- **annual-returns/** - Annual return filings
- **notices/** - Notices of change (directors, registered office, etc.)

## Common Filings

| Filing | When Required |
|--------|---------------|
| Annual Return | Within 60 days of anniversary date |
| Notice of Change of Directors | Within 15 days of change |
| Notice of Change of Registered Office | Within 15 days of change |
| Articles of Amendment | When amending articles |
