This directory contains notices filed with Corporations Canada.

Types of notices:
- Notice of Change of Directors
- Notice of Change of Registered Office
- Notice of Change of Address of Directors

Create files using format: YYYY-MM-DD-notice-type.md

Each file should link to:
- The filed notice in 09-binary-artifacts/
- Related resolution in 04-meetings-and-resolutions/
- Updated register in 03-registers/
