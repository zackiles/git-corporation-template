**EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTION PLAN**

## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Plan is to:
- Attract and retain employees, directors, and consultants
- Align participant interests with shareholders
- Provide incentive for performance

---

## 2. Definitions

- **"Board"** means the Board of Directors of the Corporation
- **"Common Shares"** means common shares in the capital of the Corporation
- **"Exercise Price"** means the price per share to exercise an Option
- **"Option"** means an option to purchase Common Shares granted under this Plan
- **"Optionee"** means a person granted an Option
- **"Plan"** means this Employee Stock Option Plan
- **"Vesting Date"** means the date on which an Option becomes exercisable

---

## 3. Administration

### 3.1 Board Authority

The Plan shall be administered by the Board, which has full authority to:
- Determine eligible participants
- Grant Options
- Set terms and conditions
- Interpret the Plan
- Make all decisions regarding the Plan

### 3.2 Committee

The Board may delegate administration to a compensation committee.

---

## 4. Shares Subject to Plan

### 4.1 Share Reserve

A maximum of [NUMBER] Common Shares are reserved for issuance under the Plan.

### 4.2 Lapsed Options

Shares subject to Options that expire or are cancelled without exercise shall be available for future grants.

---

## 5. Eligibility

Options may be granted to:
- Employees of the Corporation or its subsidiaries
- Directors of the Corporation
- Consultants providing services to the Corporation

---

## 6. Terms of Options

### 6.1 Exercise Price

The Exercise Price shall be not less than the fair market value of a Common Share on the grant date.

### 6.2 Term

Options shall expire no later than [10] years from the grant date.

### 6.3 Vesting

Options shall vest according to the schedule specified in the Option Agreement, typically:
- [25]% after [1] year of service ("cliff")
- Remaining [75]% monthly over [3] years

### 6.4 Exercise

Options may be exercised by delivery of:
- Written notice to the Corporation
- Payment of the Exercise Price
- Any required tax withholdings

---

## 7. Termination of Service

### 7.1 Voluntary Resignation

Vested Options may be exercised within [90] days of termination.

### 7.2 Termination for Cause

All Options (vested and unvested) terminate immediately.

### 7.3 Termination Without Cause

Vested Options may be exercised within [90] days of termination.

### 7.4 Death or Disability

Vested Options may be exercised within [12] months by the Optionee or estate.

---

## 8. Non-Transferability

Options are not transferable except by will or intestate succession.

---

## 9. Adjustments

### 9.1 Capital Changes

In the event of a stock split, consolidation, or similar event, the Board shall adjust the number of shares and Exercise Price.

[Describe acceleration or other treatment on change of control]

## 10. Amendment and Termination

### 10.1 Amendment

The Board may amend the Plan, subject to shareholder approval for material changes.

### 10.2 Termination

The Board may terminate the Plan at any time. Termination does not affect outstanding Options.

---

## 11. General

### 11.1 No Employment Rights

The Plan does not create any right to continued employment.
